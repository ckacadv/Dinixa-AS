How to install


24.9.2023.

After you downloaded and extracted the zip, you will see a map
called Inventar+.
Copy that folder over to the Documents folder and open it and
right click on file "Inventar+ Offline version" and click
"Create shortcut" and move "Inventar+ Offline version shortcut"
to the desktop or any location you want it to be. If you want
you can rename it to say "Inventar+" by right clicking it and
clickin "Rename"
After opening the app you have to make an account.
To do that you have to click "Add account" and type in the admin password (default is admin), after you entered
the admin password click "Add user" and enter username and
password you want.


And that's it! For instructions on how to use the app go to
"How to use Inventar+" on the sidebar
